# 🍔 Swiggy Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy sales data to uncover meaningful insights about customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance. The analysis helps understand ordering patterns, revenue distribution, and key factors influencing sales.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify customer ordering behavior
* Compare veg vs non-veg revenue contribution
* Track sales trends over time
* Identify top-performing cities

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Plotly

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** ₹5.3 Crores
* **Total Orders:** 197,430
* **Average Order Value:** ₹268.51
* **Average Rating:** 4.3 ⭐
* **Total Ratings Count:** 5.59 Million

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Total revenue generated is ₹5.3 Crores from 197K+ orders, showing strong business volume.
* Average order value is ₹268, indicating mid-range customer spending behavior.
* Average customer rating is 4.3, reflecting high customer satisfaction.
* Non-veg food contributes a larger share of revenue compared to veg items.
* Sales are highest in top-performing cities, indicating urban demand concentration.
* Quarterly analysis shows stable performance across Q1 and Q2, with a slight drop in Q3.
